******************
*** Typography ***
******************

1. Use a font size between 15 & 25 pixels for body text
2. Use really big font sizes for headlines i.e. 60px
    - may want to decrease font weight
3. Use line spacing between 120 & 150% 
4. 45 to 90 chars per line 
5. Use good fonts
    - Sans Serif
        - More neutral
        - Clean
        - Simple
        - Modern websites

        - Open Sans
        - Lato
        - Raleway
        - Monsterrat
        - PT Sans

    - Serif
        - Traditional
        - Storytelling
        - Long reading

        - Cardo
        - Merriweather
        - PT Serif

    ** Use only one at first, move to more than one after some experience* *

*************
*** Color ***
*************

1. Use only one base Color (not black, white or grey) (https://flatuicolors.com/ http://www.0to255.com/)
2. Use a tool if you want to use more colors
3. Use color to draw attention
4. Never use black in your design (almost never appears in real world so doesn't feel natural)
5. Choose colors wisely
    Red - great color to use when power, passion, strength and excitement want to be transmitted. Brighter tones are more energetic and darker shades are more powerful and elegant.

    Orange - draws attention without being as overpowering as red. It means cheerfulness and creativity. Orange can be associated with friendliness, confidence, and courage.

    Yellow - energetic and gives the feeling of happiness and liveliness. Also, it associates with curiosity, intelligence, brightness, etc.

    Green -  harmony, nature, life and health. Also, it is often associated with money. In design, green can have a balancing and harmonizing effect.

    Blue -  patience, peace, trustworthiness, and stability. It is one of the most beloved colors, especially by men. It is associated with professionalism, trust and honor. That's actually why the biggest social networks use blue.

    Purple - traditionally associated with power, nobility and wealth. In your design, purple can give a sense of wisdom, royalty, nobility, luxury, and mystery.

    Pink - expresses romance, passivity, care, peace, affection, etc.

    Brown - Brown is the color of relaxation and confidence. Brown means earthiness, nature, durability, comfort, and reliability.

**************
*** Images ***
**************

1. Put text directly on the Image
    - Works best with dark images and very light text
2. Overlay image with color/gradient to make text pop
3. Put your text in a box
    - should be opaque so you can still image behind it
4. Blur the Image or use a blurred portion of the image
5. Floor fade
    - Image fades to black at the bottom

*************
*** Icons ***
*************

1. Use icons to list features or steps
2. Use icons for actions and links
    - Should be recognizeable
    - Label if possible
3. Icons should not take a center stage
4. Use icon fonts whenever possible
    - they use vector instead of raster
    - can scale to any resoltion

**************************
*** Spacing and Layout ***
**************************

1. Use whitespace
    - between elements
    - between groups of elements
    - between sections
    - but don't exagerate
2. Define hierarchy
    - Define where you want your audience to look first
    - Establish a flow that corresponds to your content's message
    - Use whitespace to build that flow
